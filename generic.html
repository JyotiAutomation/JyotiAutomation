I am from New Delhi, India. I will start with my academics, I did my bachelor’s in computer science in 2012
followed by master’s in computer science in 2015 from India. Immediately after graduating I was hired as a data analyst.

Why I choose data analyst over front end software engineer career?
During my masters I worked on a thesis which introduced me to the machine learning algorithms and finding optimal solutions to an already existing problem. My thesis was published in IJCA, International journal of Computer Applications in 2015. It was during this thesis I developed interest towards data science and hence decided to pursue my career in the world of data.

After 2.5 years of working as data analyst I wanted to learn, understand data science in and out and hence decided to pursue another master’s, this time in data analytics. I initially started with part-time courses at OHSU, Portland in data science and then later went full-time to Northeastern University and graduated in June 2020 with a GPA of 3.87.
